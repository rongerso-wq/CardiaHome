CardiaHome

Catching heart failure before the hospital.

A daily heart check, built for the patients who need it most.

Heart failure is the number-one driver of hospital readmissions in older adults, and most are preventable, signalled by a few pounds of fluid weight gain days before the crisis. CardiaHome turns the daily weigh-in and symptom check into a simple traffic-light zone, built on real cardiology thresholds, and connects the patient to their nurse the moment something drifts. By catching trouble early and keeping patients tethered to their care team, it targets the costliest, most avoidable event in cardiac care.
